My Story

More Than a Massage — a genuine commitment to your wellbeing

Hi, I'm Anna. I've been working as a qualified massage therapist for over ten years, and my practice was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to professional, caring bodywork — not just those who can make time for a spa day.

Mobile therapy isn't a compromise. It removes the hassle of travelling while you're already tired or in discomfort, and lets you fully relax in your own space — there's no rush to drive home afterwards, and no need to prepare yourself to face the world again. You just drift.

I trained in a wide range of massage modalities and have since specialised in several areas that I feel are genuinely underserved: pregnancy massage, oncology massage, and manual lymphatic drainage. These are techniques that require additional sensitivity, specific training, and a great deal of trust between therapist and client. I take that trust seriously.

GR8BODY is a service for women — I believe in providing a safe, professional space where my clients can switch off completely. Whether you need deep muscle work after a tough week, gentle nurturing during pregnancy, or post-surgical lymphatic support, I'll tailor every session to exactly what your body needs on that day.

My Approach

Therapy That Puts you First

No two bodies are the same, and no two sessions are either. Before every treatment I take time to understand how you're feeling that day — what's tense, what aches, whether you need invigoration or deep rest. The treatment adapts to you, not the other way round.

I use only high-quality oils and professional-grade equipment. My portable massage table meets the same standards as clinic furniture, and I bring everything needed to create a calm, comfortable setting wherever you are.

GR8BODY is strictly therapeutic and non-sexual. I provide a professional service for women only — a safe and respectful environment where you can truly let go.
