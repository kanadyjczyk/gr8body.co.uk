FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you cover for mobile massage?

+

We cover South East London and Kent, including Bexleyheath, Bexley, Greenwich, Woolwich, Eltham, Abbey Wood, Sidcup, Dartford, Gravesend, Sevenoaks, Bromley, Beckenham and many more. Central London is also available. If your area isn't listed, just ask — we're always expanding.

What types of massage do you offer?

+

We offer Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, pregnancy massage, Indian head massage, manual lymphatic drainage, oncology massage, corporate chair massage and massage pamper parties. All sessions are fully therapeutic.

How long is a typical session?

+

Most sessions are 60 minutes, but 90-minute and 120-minute options are available for most treatments. Indian head massage and oncology massage are typically 45–60 minutes. Corporate chair sessions are 15–30 minutes per person.

Do I need to undress for a massage?

+

You undress to whatever level you're comfortable with. Professional draping with a sheet or towel maintains your privacy and modesty throughout the session. Your comfort is always the priority.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

+

Yes! Personalised gift vouchers are available for any treatment. They make a wonderful gift for birthdays, Mother's Day, anniversaries or any special occasion. Vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Find out more.

Is the pregnancy massage safe?

+

Yes, when performed by a trained therapist. Anna's pregnancy massage is tailored specifically to expectant mothers and is available from 15 weeks onwards. It is not suitable for high-risk pregnancies. Always consult your midwife or GP first if you have any concerns.
