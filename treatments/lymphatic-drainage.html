About This Treatment

Manual Lymphatic Drainage

Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) is a highly specialised form of gentle massage that uses precise, rhythmic movements to stimulate the lymphatic system. Unlike other massage techniques, MLD uses almost feather-light pressure — because the lymphatic vessels sit just beneath the skin. The result is a powerful treatment that helps the body eliminate waste, reduce swelling and support immune function.

Who Is This For?

MLD is particularly beneficial for those with lymphoedema, post-surgical swelling (especially after breast cancer or other procedures), chronic inflammation, fibromyalgia, sinusitis, or those who generally want to support their body's detoxification pathways. It's also used as a supportive treatment alongside oncology care.
