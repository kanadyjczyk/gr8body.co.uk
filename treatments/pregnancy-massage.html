About This Treatment

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy is one of the most transformative times in a woman's life — and it can also be one of the most physically and emotionally demanding. Anna's pregnancy massage is a specialist treatment adapted specifically for expectant mothers, using carefully modified positioning, techniques and pressure to ensure both mother and baby feel safe, supported and at ease.

Who Is This For?

Available from 15 weeks of pregnancy onwards, for mothers-to-be who are not classed as high-risk. If you're experiencing back pain, hip discomfort, swollen ankles, poor sleep, stress or anxiety during pregnancy, this treatment was made for you. Please consult your midwife or GP if you have any concerns before booking.
